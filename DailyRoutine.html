DAILY ROUTINE

ACADEMICS :

A child comes to school to learn, understand and develop. The idea is to make learning interesting and understandable to actually achieve these goals. So, the whole academic process has to be broken down properly into manageable units and then presented to the child. The school follows the curriculum of Maharashtra State board and undertakes classes from Ist to IVth standards according to the guidelines provided by the board.

FOOD IN SCHOOL :

Having food in the school gives children a break from all activities. They sit with their group of friends having the food and relax and unwind. The quality of food is superiorly taken care of by our management. We take care that students get different variety of food such that they wont get bored by similar menu everyday.

UNIFORM :

The target was to keep boys and girls at ease in their uniforms for the whole day which involved rigorous sports as well. Boys have been assigned shorts and half shirt while girls half shirt and skirt. Boys need to compulsarily have short haircut and girls with long hair may need clips, hairbands or scrunchies to keep their hair neat and in place. For girls, hair longer than shoulder length must be in plaits.

EXAMS :

Exams are conducted every month, quaterly and semi-anually. Along with written exams practical exams, assignments and projects are also regularly conducted and evaluated. The final exam is conducted at the end of year and composite marks depending upon the performance of student throughout the year are evaluated.
