  • AN APPEAL FOR DONATION

    We're a not-for-profit organisation with the goal of changing education for the better by providing a free best-in-class education for all.

    We do not charge a single penny from the student as fees or any other education related expenses. We have been providing free education for the past 153 years and strive to provide the same for many more years to come.

    The age for admission to the first standard is 5 1/2 years completed by May 31st, with a corresponding increase for other standards. We undertake admissions from Ist to IVth standards
