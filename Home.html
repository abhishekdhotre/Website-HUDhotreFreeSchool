School Overview

The H.U. Dhotre School is known for a unique work in the educational field by Dhotre family from the last 153 years. The School was established on 15th March, 1860 by Late Shri Hari Udhav Dhotre. This is a free school, co-aided and is open to everybody from the time it was established.

The School is situated in Phulgoan near Alandi(Pune) and is unique symbol of selfless service, sacrifice and social commitment by one village in India. The School is still standing tall and has motivation to carry the incredible work it has done in past 153 years for many more years to come.
